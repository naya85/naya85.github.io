Hi everyone, I'm Beatrice Caielli and I'm a designer based in Milan.

Graphic and arts have always been in my life since I was a child and I always loved anything related with shapes, colours, typography and symbols.


After classical studies in highschool and a Fashion Design bachelor degree, I've been working for 6 years, 4 of them in Berlin, Germany, travelling a lot (India, Nepal, California, England, Spain) and experiencing many different kinds of cultures, colours and tastes.


My experience includes print graphics, apparel and accessory design, websites graphic layouts, illustration and my weapons of choice are Adobe Suite CS5 and Sublime Text 2.

I've just discovered the world of coding and this is my very first website, but I really love the huge possibilities of this field, that fits very well to my personality, mixing visual imagination and logic as design and code.


Even when i'm not on the job, I enjoy expressing my creativity through an enthusiastic appreciation of computer graphic, movies, music and drawings. I also like baking.
